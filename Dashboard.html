I'd like to provide some communication tips that you can utilize while talking/chatting with the client. First Step: Before placing a BID/Contest, ensure that you have thoroughly read the project description clearly, as this will make your client want to contact you right away. Please write your proposal based on the client's description. Some wonderful tips to write great Proposal:https://www.freelancer.com.bd/support/freelancer/project/writing-a-proper-bid-proposal Second Step: Begin your communication. Hi/Hello/How are they? How are they doing, etc. Third Step: Chat with your client and ask about the project information required to give them an excellent result. And assure them that you will work on the project till they are satisfied. It will give them considerably more confidence that they are in the correct place. Fourth Step: Before you begin a project with the client, you may ask them to award you the project with a milestone. What is a milestone? How does it work? Here you may check it out from Clients side/Freelancers side: https://www.freelancer.com.bd/support/freelancer/project/writing-a-proper-bid-proposal Fifth Step: After making them happy, you could ask them to release the funds that they have created for the project. Freelancer Support: https://www.freelancer.com.bd/support/freelancer/project/writing-a-proper-bid-proposal .Click ‘Contact Us, choose your issue category, click ‘I need further assistance,’ and pick ‘I’d like to chat. Let us know if you run across any problems. Best of luck on your freelancing adventure!
