Frequently asked questions

What is the invite and earn program?

The refer and earn program allows you to earn site credit by inviting new clients to Freelancer. By participating in the program, you can earn site credit by inviting friends, family, colleagues, and other potential clients to the platform. When they sign up, they will receive $20 USD to spend on projects and contests. Once they complete $50 USD or more worth of work on Freelancer, you will receive a $20 USD credit that can be used for your next project or contest. This is a promotional offer subject to additional terms and conditions. See details.

How do I refer my friends?

You can find your unique referral link above to share however you like! All you need to do is share your link and anyone who signs up via your link will be eligible for this offer.

Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?

Currently, we are limiting the amount you can earn through this program to $1,000 USD, but you are welcome to invite as many people as you like. If you have a large social media following and would like to become an affiliate, get in touch with us at support@freelancer.com with a link to your social media account and your total reach.

Are there any restrictions on how I can use my credit?

Yes, site credit earned through the referral program is not cash and cannot be withdrawn. You may use your site credit towards purchases on Freelancer, subject to the terms and conditions of the program. See details.
